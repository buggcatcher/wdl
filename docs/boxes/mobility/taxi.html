🚕 Taxi and Uber in Florence

📞 Call a Licensed Taxi

To request a licensed taxi in Florence, you can call one of the two main taxi companies :

+39 055 4242

+39 055 4390

These are two separate companies, but both are reliable and operate throughout the city.
Operators usually speak English and are trusted for both city rides and airport transfers.


📨 Request a Taxi via Message

You can also request a taxi by sending a message via WhatsApp, SMS, or Telegram :

+39 334 662 2550 (Bookings accepted in real time or up to 24 hours in advance)



This option is especially useful for late-night rides or when phone lines are busy.


📱 Book via App

Taxis in Florence can also be booked using these mobile apps:

  • AppTaxi
  • TaxiMove



🚫 You Can’t Hail a Taxi on the Street

In Florence, you cannot hail a taxi by raising your hand or whistling. Instead:

  • Go to an official taxi stand, usually located near landmarks or train stations.

  • Book a taxi via phone, app,
    or messaging service.



⏰ Booking Tips & Seasonal Advice

During high season (May-June and September-October) and peak hours (especially around 10:00 AM, when hotel check-outs occur), taxis may be harder to find.

✅ Key information :

  • Advance bookings are allowed only one day in advance, not earlier.

  • If your requested time slot is full, your booking may be refused or rescheduled.

  • In that case, try a different time or call again closer to your departure.

  • WhatsApp or Telegram is often the most convenient and discreet way to book.

⚠️ Availability is not guaranteed during busy periods — being flexible with timing is helpful.



🎀 Pink Taxi (Taxy Rosa)

A special service for women traveling alone offers a 10% discount between:

  • 10:00 PM and 4:00 AM
📞 Dedicated number : +39 055 439000

🚖 The driver will wait until the passenger safely enters her building before leaving.
This initiative is designed to enhance women's safety during late-night travel.



🚗 Uber in Florence

Uber is available in Florence only as Uber Black, which uses licensed NCC (private chauffeur) vehicles.

Unlike standard ride-sharing :

  • It’s typically more expensive than a regular taxi.

  • Offers :
    • ✅ Upfront pricing.

    • ✅ Advance booking.

    • ✅ Professional drivers.

🚘 Availability is limited, especially outside the city center.
