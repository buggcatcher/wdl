Serre Torrigiani

Night Life in the Green Heart of Florence



The largest garden in central Florence is Boboli (40 hectares), now a public museum.

Torrigiani Garden, spanning 10 hectares, is privately owned by the Torrigiani family, who open part of it — Le Serre — to let Florence experience unforgettable nights filled with dinner, drinks, and dancing.

It’s a truly magical place, where nature and entertainment come together to create evenings full of groove.

There are several ways to enjoy Serre Torrigiani:

- Dinner + Show: Start your evening with a delicious à la carte or set menu dinner, followed by live concerts or DJ sets.

After dinner, the energy rises — especially on weekends — and the venue transforms with music and movement across both indoor and outdoor spaces.

- Just for Drinks: Come by anytime to enjoy a cocktail, wine, or beer at one of the many tables — indoors or outdoors — without needing to dine.

- Join the Party After Dinner: Arrive around 10:00 / 10:30 PM, head straight to the dance floor, and enjoy your drink while standing near the music in a vibrant setting.

The venue is large, full of charm and character — anything but ordinary.

Every corner is different, rich in atmosphere and history, offering an experience that feels both curated and spontaneous.

Expect excellent cocktails, local craft beers, fine wines, and drinks for every taste — all in an elegant, energizing environment that invites connection and celebration.
