Atomic Falafel

.Top handmade falafel in Florence, Palestinian-style.


Atomic Falafel is a vibrant street food haven nestled in the heart of Florence,
offering an authentic taste of Middle Eastern cuisine with a modern twist.

Located at Via Camillo Cavour 116r, this cozy eatery has become a favorite among locals
and travelers alike, known for its flavorful falafel and warm hospitality.


The menu showcases a delightful array of dishes, from crispy falafel wraps
and hearty hummus plates to fresh tabbouleh and savory baba ganoush.

Each item is crafted with care, using high-quality ingredients to ensure
a satisfying and wholesome meal. Vegetarian and vegan options abound,
making it an ideal spot for diverse dietary preferences.

Patrons consistently praise Atomic Falafel for its delicious offerings
and friendly service. With a welcoming atmosphere and a commitment to quality,
it's no wonder this spot has garnered a loyal following.


Whether you're exploring the city or seeking a quick, delicious bite,
Atomic Falafel promises a memorable culinary experience that
transports your taste buds to the Middle East.
