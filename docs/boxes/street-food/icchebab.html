Il Kebab dal Cuore Toscano


Icchebab Il Kebab dal Cuore Toscano is a standout culinary destination in Florence.
Offering a unique fusion of traditional Turkish kebab with authentic Tuscan flavors.
With two locations in the city, the original in Piazza Dalmazia and a second near Piazza Leopoldo,
this eatery prides itself on using locally sourced, high-quality ingredients to craft its dishes,
ensuring a fresh and flavorful experience for every customer.



The menu features a variety of options, including freshly baked paninis and piadinas
filled with tender kebab meat, crisp vegetables, and house-made sauces.
For those seeking a heartier meal, the kebab plates come generously portioned with meat,
fresh veggies, and signature sauces. Vegetarian choices are also available,
catering to diverse dietary preferences.



Icchebab's commitment to quality and authenticity has earned it high praise from patrons.
Boasting a 4.9 out of 5 rating on platforms like Restaurant Guru and Tripadvisor.
Customers frequently highlight the exceptional taste and the dedication of the staff
in delivering a memorable dining experience.



Whether you're a local or a visitor exploring Florence, Icchebab offers a delightful culinary
journey that combines the rich traditions of Tuscan and Turkish cuisines.
