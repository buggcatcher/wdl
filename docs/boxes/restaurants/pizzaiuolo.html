Pizzaiuolo

If you’re craving an authentic Neapolitan pizza experience in Florence, Il Pizzaiuolo is the perfect destination—wood-fired pies made with buffalo mozzarella DOP, San Marzano tomato, and extra‑virgin olive oil in a warm, family‑style setting near the Sant’Ambrogio market.
