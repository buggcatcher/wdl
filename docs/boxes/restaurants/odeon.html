Caffè Odeon - Where Tuscan Charm Meets Theatrical Flair

Step into this 1922 theater-turned-bistro nestled within Palazzo Strozzi, where refined Tuscan cuisine meets spectacular cocktails. Vaulted ceilings, outdoor seating, and the mysterious wine cellar... an experience unlike any other!

Come for the exquisite ingredients, stay for the creative cocktails. Enjoy the show!
