#RAW

If you're looking for high-quality products and healthy, nourishing recipes, RAW is the perfect place to take care of your body with flavor.
Run by a team of passionate vegan women, this small yet thoughtfully designed spot radiates calm and well-being, with both indoor and outdoor seating, an open kitchen, and a fully raw vegan menu.
The creative offerings include wraps, tacos, pizzas, smoothies, cold-pressed juices, ice creams, desserts, and much more—100% vegan and 100% raw.
That means everything is made with uncooked ingredients or gently prepared below 42°C to preserve vitamins and enzymes.
The dishes feature house-made plant-based cheeses, seasonal produce, and a touch of the exotic.
