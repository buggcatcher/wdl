La Casalinga

Trattoria La Casalinga - Homestyle Tuscan Cooking Since 1963

Located on Via dei Michelozzi, Trattoria La Casalinga has been serving traditional Tuscan dishes in a warm, family-friendly setting for over half a century. Known for its generous portions and reasonable prices, it's a favorite among locals and tourists alike.


distance from Duomo : 1,2km
